DEVELOPED BY A PASTOR FOR PASTORS (AND OTHER CHURCH LEADERS)

We know the struggles church leaders face regarding effective, efficient communication. You want to make sure to touch the lives of every single person that walks into your church, but you wear an insane number of hats! You have probably, like me, felt discouraged at times, knowing that another first-time guest "slipped through the cracks."

That's why we developed The Church Member's Journey. We wanted a way to clearly and consistently shepherd everyone who came into our church from the moment of first contact to church membership and beyond. We desire to equip you with the tools and processes you need to consistently invite your community into a relationship with your church as well.
